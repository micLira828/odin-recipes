Basic Omelette

Equipment

  • Frying Pan
  • Bowl
  • Spatula

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil or butter
  • 2 or 3 eggs
  • milk
  • Extras, like:
    • Salsa
    • Vegetables
    • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the pan
  2. Place olive oil or butter on pan and swerve around until spread
  3. Break two eggs into a bowl, and throw away shells
  4. Pour a little bit of milk from the carton into the bowl
  5. Pour the mixture into the pan
  6. gradually pour/put in the extra ingredients
  7. When it gets not liquid, flip it over with a spatula
