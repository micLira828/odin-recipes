Grilled Cheese Sandwitch

Equipment

  • Frying Pan
  • Spatula

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Any additional ingredients(like tomatoes or chicken
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Pour oil or butter on pan and swerve around
  2. Preheat Pan
  3. Put cheese in between two bread slices, and any of your additional ingredients
  4. Put sandwitch into pan
  5. After a few minutes, when one side of the bread is brown, flip it over
